Bohemian Rhapsody Queen

Everything in your notch.

Ten powerful features, all accessible from the space you never knew you had.

Media Controls

Now Playing info, album art, playback controls. See what's playing at a glance.

File Shelf

Drag files to the notch for temporary storage. Drag out to any app. AirDrop with a drop.

System Monitor

CPU, RAM, Network, Battery. Real-time metrics in your notch.

Calendar

Upcoming events at a glance. Never miss a meeting.

Clipboard History

Every copy, searchable and accessible. Pin important entries.

Custom HUDs

Replace macOS volume and brightness popups with beautiful notch HUDs.

Quick Apps

Pin your favorite apps. Launch with one click.

Camera Mirror

Quick mirror check right from the notch.

Apple Shortcuts

Trigger your favorite Shortcuts from the notch.

Multi-Monitor

Works on every display. Notch and non-notch screens.

Effortlessly intuitive.

Three gestures. Infinite possibilities.

1

Hover to expand

Move your cursor over the notch. Cornice expands into a full command surface.

2

Click to switch

Click a tab to switch between features. Media, shelf, monitor, calendar, and more.

3

Drag to shelf

Drag any file to the notch. It stays on the shelf until you need it.
